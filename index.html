Company Info

About the Company

Watch Station partners with the world’s most iconic brands to curate a unique collection of the newest designer watches and smartwatches for men and women. Since joining Fossil Group in 2007, we now operate around 100 stores worldwide and offer a robust online and in-store experience that connects you to the stories, trends and latest innovations in the world of watches and wearable tech.

Branding

Time Well Spent: We’re here to help you find the right watch for your individual style. Whether you’re marking a life milestone or needing something to simply tell you when you’re running late, we believe a watch should be just as meaningful as your time is.

Target

Watch Station carries a wide variety of brands ranging from mid to high-end products at an outlet price. Thus their target age is from 25-49, with a slightly above average income. Their consumers are fashion forward and are usually not buying watches from Watch Station as a long-term investment, but rather to satisfy current styling needs.
